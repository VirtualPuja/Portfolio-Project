About Me

Hi I’m Puja. My motto in life is to never settle whether it’s professionally or personally. Because life is too short to be in a situation that makes you unhappy.
The majority of my professional experience is in real estate law. I worked at multiple real estate title companies in Chicago and Cincinnati as well as a paralegal at a well-established law practice. The reason I loved my profession is that it involved analytical thinking, problem solving, and multi-tasking. You may be wondering the reason I decided to leave the industry. Well there wasn’t room for growth and I didn’t feel like I reached my full potential.
Hence, I decided to embark on a journey to become a front-end developer. I have always had an interest in learning more about programming languages as well as learning the fundamentals of building websites/web applications.I believe that this field offers endless opportunities for growth, innovation, and creativity, and I am excited to be a part of it.
In my free time, you can find me exploring the city with my husband and dog, working out, or experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen. I am a firm believer in work-life balance and enjoy spending quality time with my loved ones.
